Saad Lamjarred

Saad Lamjarred is a Moroccan pop singer-songwriter, multi-instrumentalist, dancer, record producer and actor.

He grew up in an artist family and became famous after his songs broke YouTube audience records, making him a major figure in Amazigh and Arabic pop, and the first singer to popularize Moroccan pop.
